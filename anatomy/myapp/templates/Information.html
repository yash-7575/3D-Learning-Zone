Amazing Organs of the Human Body

The Heart: Your Body's Powerful Pump

The heart is a muscular organ about the size of your fist that works tirelessly to pump blood throughout your body.

  • Beats about 100,000 times per day
  • Pumps about 2,000 gallons of blood daily
  • Has four chambers: two atria and two ventricles
  • Contains special electrical cells that help it beat in rhythm

Fun Fact!

Your heart creates enough energy daily to drive a truck for 20 miles!

The Brain: Your Body's Control Center

The brain is like a super-computer that controls everything you do, think, and feel.

  • Contains about 86 billion nerve cells
  • Uses 20% of your body's energy
  • Processes information at up to 268 mph
  • Is protected by the skull and three layers of protective tissue

Fun Fact!

Your brain can generate enough electricity to power a small light bulb!

The Lungs: Your Breathing Machines

Your lungs are amazing organs that help you breathe and get oxygen to every part of your body.

  • You take about 20,000 breaths per day
  • Contains about 300 million tiny air sacs called alveoli
  • Right lung is slightly larger than the left
  • Can hold about 6 liters of air when fully inflated

Fun Fact!

If you laid out all the airways in your lungs in a line, they would stretch for about 1,500 miles!

The Liver: Your Body's Chemical Factory

The liver is your largest internal organ and performs hundreds of vital functions.

  • Processes everything you eat and drink
  • Produces proteins for blood plasma
  • Stores extra nutrients
  • Helps clean your blood of harmful substances

Fun Fact!

Your liver is the only organ that can regenerate itself! It can regrow to full size even if up to 75% is removed.

The Kidneys: Your Body's Filtering System

Your kidneys are bean-shaped organs that clean your blood and help control blood pressure.

  • Filter about 120-150 quarts of blood every day
  • Remove waste and extra water from your body
  • Help make red blood cells
  • Keep your bones strong by helping process vitamin D

Fun Fact!

Your kidneys filter all the blood in your body about 40 times every day!
